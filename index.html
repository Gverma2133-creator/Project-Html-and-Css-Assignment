Biography

Nikola Tesla was a Serbian-American inventor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, and futurist who is best known for his contributions to the design of the modern alternating current (AC) electricity supply system. He was born on July 10, 1856, and died on January 7, 1943.

"The present is theirs; the future, for which I really worked, is mine."
